

### **1. Login/Registration Screen**
- **Purpose**: Allow users (dieticians, admins, or patients) to log in or register.
- **Key Features**:
  - Login with email/phone and password.
  - Registration form for new users (patients or dieticians).
  - Password reset option.

---

### **2. Dashboard**
- **Purpose**: Provide a high-level overview of activities.
- **Key Features**:
  - Upcoming appointments or follow-ups.
  - Alerts for new patient inquiries or updates.
  - Quick links to commonly used features (e.g., Add Patient, View Diet Plans).

---

### **3. Patient Management**
#### **a. Patient List Screen**
- **Purpose**: View and manage all patients.
- **Key Features**:
  - Search bar and filters (by name, age, or last visit).
  - A summary card for each patient with basic details (e.g., name, last visit, next appointment).
  - Action buttons (e.g., "View Details," "Add Visit").

#### **b. Patient Details Screen**
- **Purpose**: View detailed information about a specific patient.
- **Key Features**:
  - Tabs or sections for:
    - **Personal Details**: Name, DOB, contact info.
    - **Health Details**: Allergies, medical history, lifestyle.
    - **Clinical Visits**: List of visits with quick access to visit notes and metrics.
    - **Diet Plans**: Current and past diet plans.
  - Action buttons for adding/editing visits, diet plans, or notes.

---

### **4. Clinical Visits**
#### **a. Add/Edit Visit Screen**
- **Purpose**: Record new visits or edit past visit details.
- **Key Features**:
  - Form to input visit details (date, reason, diagnoses, notes).
  - Option to upload documents (e.g., prescriptions, lab reports).
  - Fields for health metrics (e.g., blood pressure, BMI).

#### **b. Visit Details Screen**
- **Purpose**: View detailed records of a specific visit.
- **Key Features**:
  - Summary of diagnoses, prescriptions, and metrics.
  - Follow-up date and notes from the visit.

---

### **5. Diet Plan Management**
#### **a. Create/Edit Diet Plan Screen**
- **Purpose**: Enable dieticians to create personalized diet plans for patients.
- **Key Features**:
  - Select meal preferences and dietary restrictions.
  - Customize meals for breakfast, lunch, dinner, and snacks.
  - Set duration of the diet plan (e.g., 1 week, 1 month).

#### **b. Diet Plan Overview Screen**
- **Purpose**: View current and past diet plans.
- **Key Features**:
  - Display diet plans in a calendar or list format.
  - Option to mark a diet plan as "completed" or "inactive."
  - Export diet plan to PDF or email.

---

### **6. Appointment Scheduling**
- **Purpose**: Manage appointments between dieticians and patients.
- **Key Features**:
  - Calendar view with patient appointment slots.
  - Option to schedule, reschedule, or cancel appointments.
  - Notifications for upcoming appointments.

---

### **7. Alerts and Notifications**
- **Purpose**: Display system-generated alerts and reminders.
- **Key Features**:
  - Alerts for overdue follow-ups, API health (heartbeat check), or critical patient metrics.
  - Notification center with categorized alerts.

---

### **8. Reports and Analytics**
- **Purpose**: Provide insights into patient progress and system performance.
- **Key Features**:
  - Patient progress reports (e.g., weight change, adherence to diet plans).
  - Clinical visit summaries.
  - Application-level reports for admins (e.g., active patients, usage stats).

---

### **9. Admin Panel**
- **Purpose**: For dieticians or admins to configure application settings.
- **Key Features**:
  - Manage users (add/edit dieticians or patients).
  - Set system thresholds (e.g., API health monitoring).
  - View logs of recent system activity.

---

### **10. Help/Support Screen**
- **Purpose**: Assist users with FAQs or direct support.
- **Key Features**:
  - FAQs for using the application.
  - Contact form for raising support tickets.

---
